About IDR

Where Academic Rigour Meets Industry Reality

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute built to address the growing shortage of skilled professionals in digital, cyber and AI risk. We operate at the intersection of regulated industry and higher education — partnering with leading UK universities to deliver programmes that reflect the genuine challenges organisations face in high-consequence environments.

Our model goes beyond the classroom. By embedding academic insight directly into live industry projects, we ensure every practitioner we develop is deployment-ready from day one — whether that means managing a cyber incident, implementing a risk framework, or advising on AI governance policy.
