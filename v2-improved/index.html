About IDR

Where Academic Rigour Meets Industry Reality

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute addressing the growing shortage of skilled professionals in digital, cyber and AI risk. We partner with leading UK universities to design programmes that reflect the genuine challenges organisations face in high-consequence environments.

Our model goes beyond the classroom — embedding academic insight directly into live industry projects so every practitioner we develop is deployment-ready from day one.

What We Do

Three Pillars, One Integrated Model

IDR delivers end-to-end capability across education, innovation and advisory — designed to work as one connected system.

01
🎓

Academy

Structured bootcamps and professional programmes for students and career-changers. Curriculum co-designed with industry partners to address real gaps in cyber and AI risk talent.

02
💡

Innovation & Incubation

Research into emerging risk frameworks, AI governance models and digital resilience. We develop proprietary IP and methodologies that keep organisations ahead of evolving threats.

03
📊

Expert guidance for implementing NIST CSF, ISO 27001 and NIS2. We translate complex regulatory obligations into practical programmes that build lasting operational resilience.
